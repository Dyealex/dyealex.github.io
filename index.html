What about me?

What can I say about me? I have been with my husband 36 years. That is longer than a lot of people in this class have been on the earth. I have been a press operator for 36 years as well. Technology has now taken over my job so I am trying to get into technology. Technology has been taking over jobs since the beginning of time so why should mine be different? There are no jobs in my field so I will soon be forced out. I would have been out a few years ago if I worked for anyone but the man I work for.

My husband and I have no children, only pets. 12 cats, 3 dogs and a turtle. To see all our "children" click here. That turtle is suppose to belong to my great niece but she was scared of it when it was the size of oversized quarter. I can barely get my hand around his fat, overfed shell now so chances are pretty good that she will never want this turtle back. He has outgrown four aquariums and is close to outgrowing #5!

How did I decide I would like to try coding for fun? Pure, dumb luck. I contacted a friend of a friend and he told me about it. It was all new to me at the time but I looked into it and decided to give it a try. Since November or so, I have spent a great deal of time and energy on it but still feel totally lost! The hours of Udemy have paid off but not to the degree that I had hoped.

My free time for the past 3 plus years have been occupied with training for Ironman races. The original plan was to do one and be done but that did not work out quite as planned. I did the third and FINAL Ironman race on Oct. 13th, 2019. I finished but barely. For the complete boring story of my Ironman quest see My Ironman Journey...

In my limited free time, I dabble in stained glass. After trying mosaics, it is far more forgiving and less stressfull than the glass work. Since I do so little of it and so seldom, I can't get good at the soldering. Crafters are pretty harsh critics of their own work. And if there is an imperfection in the solder, I will ruin the whole piece trying to fix it. The cutting of the glass has to pretty precise to fit properly and I can't get the hang of it and spend a lot of time recutting piecses.

Ultimately, I would like to have a nav bar at the top with tabs and but this will have to do for now. I am working on getting pictures of all the pets but weather was so bad Sunday that I did not get to it.
